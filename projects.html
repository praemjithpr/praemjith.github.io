AI based Hexacopter

The AI Hexacopter is an advanced autonomous drone built for search and rescue missions, equipped with human detection, payload delivery, and real-time communication. It uses a Raspberry Pi with Camera V2 and a thermal camera for vision processing, while ArduPilot controls autonomous navigation through GPS waypoints. LoRa modules enable long-range communication with the ground control station, transmitting location data after detections. The system integrates obstacle avoidance, night vision, and an FPV camera for live coverage, along with a disaster prediction app powered by IoT sensors.
